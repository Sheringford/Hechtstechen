Travel and Contact

The venue of the tournament is the St. Pauli Salon at the Hechtstraße 32 in Dresden. It is in walking distance of the train station "Dresden Bahnhof Neustadt".

Dresden has a small airport, however finding a suitable direct flight might be difficult. If you want to fly in, you might also want to consider booking to Berlin, Leipzig/Halle, or Prague. All of those places are well connected to Dresden via train and bus.

There are numerous places to stay at in walking distance of the venue. Here a few examples which might worth considering:

- an affordable hotel nearby is the Best Western Macrander

- cheaper but also with a bit worse ratings: ibis Styles

- if you something that is closer to a backpacker hostel, you might want to check out the Gästehaus Mezcalero

If you have any questions regarding the tournament or travelling and accomodation, feel free to write me at robschuppe@posteo.de!
